U1NW_FD: This dataset contains data collected from User 1 on Day 1, during which the participant performed walking trials using their normal walking style under consistent conditions.

U2NW_FD: This dataset contains data collected from the same user (User 1) on Day 2, again following their normal walking pattern to ensure comparability across sessions.

In total, each participant contributed approximately 12 minutes of walking data, collected over two separate days to capture potential variations in gait while maintaining overall consistency in walking behavior.
